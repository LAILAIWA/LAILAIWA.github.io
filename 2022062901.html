Thinking in English

  1. Name objects around you in English.Think obout pronunciation.YouGlish（搜索发音网站）Every time you`re in a new room/environment, take a few seconds to do it.The context will help you to remember them.
  2. Think in simple sentences.
    • I`m XXX
    • The drawer is open. My desk is empty.
    • If there`s something you can`t describe, look up the words you need in a dictionary, memorize it
    • Get an English-only dictionary rather than a translating dictionary between English and your native language.We`re building your mind to work in English mode rather than translation mode.（Merriam-Webster）
  3. Hava small conversations with yourself in English.Set aside 2 minutes every day to have a simple conversation with yourself.Do this every day for 30 days. Don`t take a day off.
  4. Change at least one of your everyday life things to English.
    • Switch your calendar to English.Use the English months and days of the week.
    • To-do list. A grocery list. write your`re going to be doing in English.
    • Set your App language to English.
    • Read an English newspaper, or listen to news in English，try write something in Englsih.
    • When you do something like getting dressed、cleaning up，As you`re doing it, in your head, narrate in English.
    • Learn something only in English.
  5. Keep track so you`re doing it every day.

发音

  • 清音：发音时声带（喉结下面一点）不振动的音，如/t/
  • 浊音：发音时声带振动的音，如/d/

1 单词结尾的S

  • /s/：单词最后一个音是清音
    • /p/：shop-shops
    • /t/：cat-cats
    • /k/：week-weeks
    • /f/：brief-briefs
  • /z/：单词最后一个音是浊音
    • /b/ 、/d/、/g/、/l/、/r/：cube-cubes，bed-beds，dog-dogs，
    • /w/、/m/、/n/、/v/、/y/ ：come-comes，corn-corns，wave-waves
  • /iz/：单词结尾是以S /s/ 、Z /z/ 、sh /ʃ/ 、ch /tʃ/ 、dg /dʒ/ 这些音结尾
    • /s/：bus-buses
    • /z/：raise-raises
    • /ʃ/：push-push-es
    • /tʃ/：bench-benches
    • /dʒ/：page-pages
  • 特殊：单词以S结尾，但发音为/z/，如is、His、because、easy、please

2 W和V发音区别

  • W字母发音：Double U
  • W单词发音：嘴唇呈亲吻状，并送气发音
    • why、what、when、swing、swap、sweet
  • V单词发音：上排牙齿紧靠下嘴唇，并送气发音
    • very、Vincent、victory、every
  • 练习：
    • vest - west
    • visor - wiser
    • viper - wiper
    • verse - worse
    • vine - wine

3 容易搞混的TH

  • 对比如下单词发音区别：
    • Thank - sank
    • Think - sink
    • Thick - sick
    • Thing - sing
  • TH发音：舌头在上下牙齿间，然后送气发音
    • 清音/θ/：咬住舌头，送气发音
    • 浊音/ð/：咬住舌头，声带振动（兹）
    • 规则：
      1. 当th位于单词词首，判断词性。
        • 实词（名词、动词、形容词、数字词）发清音：thing、throat、Thursday、think、thank、throw、thick、thirsty、thoughtful、three、third。
        • 虚词（功能词、代词、连接词）发浊音：this、those、then、than、their、there、them、they、these、therefore、though
      2. 当th位于单词词中：
        • 如果后面接er或者最后一个字母为不发音的e，发浊音：feather、weather、leather、further、father、bathe、breathe、loathe
        • 其他情况发清音：truthful、faithful、method、nothing、wealthy
      3. 当th位于单词词尾，大多数为清音，极少数个例发浊音如with。
  • S发音：不需要咬舌头，牙齿可以挨着，与中文S一致

4 ed结尾发音

  • /t/：单词尾音是清音
    • p - helped、stoped
    • k - asked、talked、walked
    • f - sniffed、cuffed
    • gh - laughed
    • sh - washed
    • ch - watched
    • ss - kissed
    • c - danced
    • x - fixed
  • /d/：单词尾音是浊音
    • v：loved
    • m：doomed
    • n：learned
    • s：used
    • r：fired
  • /id/：单词以/t/或/d/两个音结尾
    • t - wanted、visited
    • d - needed、handed

5 L发音

  • 日韩母语没有L，会和R分不清。
  • /l/音标只有一个，但发音有两个：
    • light l：单词开头或单词中间，嘴巴张开，下巴下移同时舌头上移，舌尖自然接触上排牙齿背面位置，然后发音（先抬舌头再发音）：listen、like、live、blow、fly、follow。
    • dark l：单词词尾，把舌头抬起来的过程发音（先发音再抬舌头）：still、cool、pull、feel、school、bell。

三. 学习技巧

3.1 影子跟读

有助于口语，加强发音、语调和语速方面的练习，也可以提高听力理解能力。

  1. 找到一段适合自己当前英语水平的音频，可以是短对话、听力素材、有声新闻、影视剧片段等。
  2. 播放时注意听清发音和语调，并理解意思。
  3. 播放后马上跟读这段内容，尽量模仿原音。
  4. 反复进行跟读练习，多次练习同一段音频，直到自己可以流利、自然的说出为止。

3.2 自然拼读法

自然拼读法（Phonics）指看到一个英语单词，就可以根据英文字母在单词里的发音规律把这个单词念出来的一种方法。它通过建立字母或字母组合与词形之间的对应关系，即词汇的各种拼读规律，帮助学生读、写和记忆词汇。

儿童学习语言的顺序是听、说、读、写，积累了一定量发音的词汇量后并不意味着能够“识字”，自然拼读法是串联发音和单词拼写的桥梁。但中国的英语教育中儿童是从阅读开始，没有前置的词汇积累阶段。

四. 纯正英式短语&句型1000条

4.1 话题-教育

sth. be an arduous process

  • 中文含义：某事是一个需要付出艰苦努力的过程

  • 同义：= sth. be a tiring process that requires a great deal of effort

  • 第二语言者倾向表达：sth. is an exhausting thing to do.

  • 实战例句：Attending a university with a top-tier reputation often is an arduous but rewarding process.

    考入一所名牌大学通常是一次需要付出艰苦努力但回报也同样丰厚的过程。

  • 联想句型：

    1. be very rewarding

      有回报的 = making someone feel satisfied

      Teaching can be a very rewarding career. It allows teachers to have an input in the development of young lives.

    2. someone`s aspiration / pursuit

      梦想，追求（代替dream的地道表达）= someone`s strong wish for achievement

      The educational aspirations / pursuits of rural youth tend to be different from those of students living elsewhere.

concentrate on sth.

  • 中文含义：集中精力于某事

  • 同义：= direct someone`s thoughts or attention towards sth.

  • 实战例句：Some people think it is more effective to educate boys and girls in single-sex schools because they believe this environment reduces distractions and encourages pupils to concentrate on their studies.

    一些人认为在男校或女校教育孩子更为有效，因为单一性别环境可以减少干扰并鼓励孩子们集中精力地学习

  • 联想句型：

    1. form the basic of sth.

      为某事物打下基础 = provide the ideas or actions from which sth. can develop

      Traditional ways of teaching form the basic of the lesson.

use lateral thinking

  • 中文含义：鼓励发散型思维

  • 同义：= use a creative approach to solving problems

  • 第二语言者倾向表达：think in a creative way

  • 实战例句：Using lateral thinking, the students can solve problems in an unconventional manner now.

    通过利用发散式思维，学生们可以用不走寻常路的方式来解决问题。

  • 联想句型：

    1. rely on linear thinking

      依赖于直线型的思维；受到思维局限 = depend on habits of thought in which one idea follows directly from another one

      The students should not rely on linear thinking habits. Instead, they should think more innovatively.

    2. break the mould

      打破常规 = change a situation by doing sth. unprecedented

      He made an attempt to break the mould of British politics.

academic performance

  • 中文含义：学业表现

  • 同义：= how good or bad a student is academically

  • 第二语言者倾向表达：grades

  • 实战例句：Tyler`s academic performance has been disappointing.

    泰勒的学习表现十分令人失望。

  • 联想句型：

    1. academic qualifications

      学历 = the degrees, diplomas and certificates that someone has acquired.

      Today academic qualifications are essential for getting a reasonable career.

    2. standardised test

      标准化考试 = tests that are designed to give each test-taker the same questions in the same format.

      High scores on standardised tests are not necessarily the best indicator of giftedness.

      If primary language teaching is not standardised, secondary schools could be faced with a great variety of levels in different languages within their intake.

    3. be gifted / be talented

      有天赋的 = has an impressive natural ability

      In the process of being educated we can learn from the achievements of those more gifted than ourselves/
